About Us

Cococreative is a UMKM that is engaged in manufacturing where we process products from coconut coir waste into handicrafts, decorations, and planting media. Cococreative is one of the leading coconut coir handicraft product manufacturers in Indonesia. We have been around for more than 6 months in derivative products. We produce turus or so-called plant supports, flower pots, and vases with several variants, coco fiber, cocopeat, and coconut coir ropes.

At this time we have more than 50 employees and more than 50 coconut craftsmen and farmers who work for the satisfaction and expectations of our customers and are encouraged by some of the nation's best innovative technologiess
